# Farmer Cooperative Crop Supply System (FCCSS)

## Database Objective

**Database Name:** **FCCSS — Farmer Cooperative Crop Supply System**

The **Farmer Cooperative Crop Supply System (FCCSS)** is designed to digitize and manage the complete agricultural supply chain, from farmer crop registration to the final delivery of produce to retailers and wholesalers.

The system enables cooperative societies to efficiently procure, inspect, process, store, and distribute agricultural products while maintaining transparency in farmer payments, logistics, and government subsidy management.

### Key Business Activities

- Farmer registration and crop listing
- Cooperative society management
  - Crop procurement
  - Quality inspection
  - Pricing
  - Inventory management
  - Distribution
- Logistics management
  - Vehicle allocation
  - Farm-to-warehouse delivery tracking
- Retail and wholesale order fulfillment
- Government subsidy tracking for farmer input subsidies
- Financial reporting
  - Profit/Loss analysis per crop lot
  - Farmer payment tracking
  - Commission management

---

## Application Users

The FCCSS database supports the following stakeholders:

### Farmers
- Register crop details
- View procurement offers
- Track payments
- Monitor government subsidy credits

### Cooperative Society Administrators
The primary users of the system responsible for:
- Procurement management
- Quality inspections
- Inventory management
- Crop pricing
- Distribution to buyers

### Logistics Partners
- Transport crops from farms to cooperative warehouses
- Track delivery status and vehicle allocation

### Retailers
- Purchase processed and graded crops in smaller quantities from the cooperative

### Wholesalers
- Purchase agricultural produce in bulk directly from cooperative inventory

### Government / Subsidy Authority
- Record farmer subsidy schemes
- Track subsidy disbursements
- Maintain subsidy-related records

### System Administrator
- Manage user accounts
- Configure system settings
- Generate reports and monitor overall system performance
